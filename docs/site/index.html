System overview

The Substrate is a mini Netflix-style ads platform — four small services producing real traffic, logs, metrics, and stageable incidents. The Nexus is the AI foundation that operates on it: a grounded context layer, development and operations agents, a multi-agent orchestrator, and an eval harness that publishes numbers for every capability.

Level 0 — The Substrate (Days 1–5)

Goal: a small but production-shaped ads platform that gives every later AI capability something real to operate on — real traffic to observe, real logs and metrics to correlate, real incidents to diagnose. No mock-data theater.

Decisions so far

ADR-0001: Docker Compose over Kubernetes

Four services run by one developer on one machine. Compose gives one-command startup and reproducible demos; Kubernetes would add operational overhead while proving nothing relevant to AI foundation engineering. Full ADR
